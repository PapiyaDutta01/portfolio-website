About Me

I am a Professor of Electronics & Communication Engineering with over 23 years of experience spanning teaching, research, academic administration, accreditation, innovation, and industry collaboration. My research interests focus on 5G/6G Communications, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Cognitive Radio Networks, IoT, Wireless Sensor Networks, and Intelligent Communication Systems. Throughout my academic career, I have successfully led multiple government-funded research projects sponsored by AICTE, DST-SERB, and other national agencies. As Principal Investigator and Co-Principal Investigator. I have secured and managed research grants exceeding ₹50 Lakhs while fostering interdisciplinary innovation and industry-academia collaboration.
